About Me 🔥

Born into the digital era, technology has changed immensely since I first started using it. I remember playing my gameboy color, and to think how far things have progressed. I've always had an inclination for tinkering and breaking technology, and I began programming when I was 17 years old, honing skills from sites like Codecademy and Udemy.

I then enrolled in Gettysburg College, declaring a computer science undergraduate degree when I first arrived. Since then, I have worked on interesting projects, which you can check out in my portfolio below.

Earning my liberal arts bachelor's degree with a major in computer science and minor in economics has shaped the way I approach the world. Analyzing websites, information, and applications has become second nature to me, and I am always reading about new trends and developments.

I was born and bred in New York City, and in my free time support the Knicks, enjoy making food, and attempt to take photos.
